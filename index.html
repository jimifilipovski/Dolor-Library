Dolor Library

The Library is a personal consortment of titles that have had a meaningful impact in my life. "If you only read the books that everyone else is reading, you can only think what everyone else is thinking." –Haruki Murakami

Mastery

I’m a fan of biographies and Mastery is a collection of a dozen ones ranging from Charles Darwin, Benjamin Franklin, Albert Einstein to Leonard da Vinci. Robert Greene takes apart some of these success stories and tries to make sense of the myth behind becoming a true master.

The Art Of Looking Sideways

Alan Fletcher's primer of visual intelligence. This book weighs a whopping 6 pounds and is meant to be read in a non-linear fashion. I often flip through the pages of this book with my morning coffee to get inspired and it truly helps me to see things in a different way.
